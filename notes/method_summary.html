1. Survival curves were fitted using Kaplan-Meier estimator
  2. Curves were compared for different factors: home, innings, top team and position
  3. Log-rank test confirmed that only home, innings and top team were statistically significant
  4. Cox proportions hazards regression model was fitted for each factor and in combination
  5. Schoenfeld and deviance residuals revealed no outliers, no violation of the proportional hazards assumption and that the factors were statistically significant
  6. Metrics were created to compare players on consistency and performances in home / away conditions against top teams
  7. Cluster analysis revealed five distinct clusters that separated the great from the good
